Ingredients:
	2 eggs
	1 cup of water
	1 green onions
	salt

Directions:
 1. Add two eggs and stir.
 
 2. In a large bowl, add salt, water, and finely chopped green onions.
 
 3. Prepare a flat pot, add water so that the bowl, when put in the pot, is immersed up to 10% of its height.

 4. Apply low heat and boil. The egg will swell and get firm. Use a chopstic to poke the egg to check its firmness.
